# On-Site Traffic Optimization Research Prompt

**Research Goal:** Identify all effective ON-SITE methods for generating qualified organic website traffic in 2025-2026, focusing exclusively on optimizations that can be implemented directly on the website itself.

**IMPORTANT CONSTRAINT:** Research ONLY methods that can be implemented ON THE WEBSITE. Exclude external channels like social media marketing, paid advertising, email marketing, influencer outreach, etc.

**Focus:** Website code, content, structure, technical optimizations, and on-page SEO/GEO strategies.

---

## Research Parameters

**Time Frame:** Focus on 2025-2026 data and trends (last 12 months)
**Source Priority:**
1. Academic research papers (arXiv, ACM, IEEE)
2. Industry research reports (Gartner, Forrester, HubSpot Research)
3. Data-driven case studies with metrics
4. Expert analyses from verified professionals
5. Official platform documentation (Google, Meta, etc.)

**Exclude:**
- Generic listicles, outdated (pre-2024) advice, opinion pieces without data
- Off-site strategies (social media, paid ads, email, partnerships, etc.)
- Methods requiring external platforms or third-party dependencies

- Industry-specific effectiveness variations
