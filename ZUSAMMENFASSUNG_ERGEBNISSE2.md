## 11. ADVANCED TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATIONS

### Robots.txt
- Block: Low-Value, Duplicate, Admin, Infinite Spaces
- AI Crawler: OpenAI, Microsoft, Google-Extended, etc.
- Limitation: Verhindert Crawling, nicht Indexing

### Canonical Tags
- Self-Referencing auf jeder Seite
- Cross-Domain für Syndicated Content

### Pagination
- Self-Referencing Canonicals
- rel="next/prev"
- Clean URLs
